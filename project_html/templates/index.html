The Misdiagnosis Problem

According to this article in ScienceDaily, patients are often "misdiagnosed with pneumonia at an alarming rate," leading to an overuse of antibiotics and an increase in healthcare costs.

Pneumonia is second only to congestive heart failure "as the reason for readmission within 30 days of a previous hospitalization," according to researchers led by Henry Ford Infectious Diseases physicians Hiren Pokharna, M.D., and Norman Markowitz, M.D. This study found that "72 percent of patients were misdiagnosed with pneumonia upon readmission to the same hospital." Most of these misdiagnoses happened in the emergency department.

Dr. Pokharna noted that this analysis "points to the importance of using X-ray for ruling out pneumonia."


Machine Learning: A Solution for Improving Diagnosis Rates

A fast and accurate resource for analyzing x-rays has the potential to drastically reduce the rates of pneumonia misdiagnosis, especially in emergency departments. Machine learning provides a solution to this problem. A deep learning model is capable of reading x-ray images and classifying them as pneumonia or not pneumonia.
